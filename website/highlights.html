Amdo.1.Ellobius

Meta.1.Sauria is - we think - the oldest EVE ever described. We identified putatively orthologous copies in a lizard - the tuatara (Sphenodon) - as well as in multiple avian species.

This insertion also provides important new insights into the evolution of parvoviruses. It belongs to the recently described Metaparvovirus genus. So far, metaparvoviruses have only been identifiied in fish. Unexpectedly, these fish viruses were found to group relatively closely with mammalian parvoviruses (genus Orthoparvovirus). This seemed to imply a transfer of viruses between fish and mammals!
